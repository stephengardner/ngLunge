help_outline Certifications FAQ

What certifications does Lunge support?
Lunge supports most of the major industry certifications. If you feel that there is a certification that Lunge should add, contact us at certifications@lunge.io.
What does being "Verified" mean?
Lunge takes pride in providing the public with accurate information on professionals near them. Being verified means Lunge has independently verified that your certification is valid and up-to-date. Your certification status will update from "Pending" to "Verified" and that information will be reflected on your profile.
Does being Verified matter?
Absolutely. Being verified should be one of the first steps in creating a Lunge Trainer profile. Users will be able to find you more easily, you will be ranked higher in search results, and you will be trusted more by having the Verified (verified_user) badge on your profile.
How can I be Verified with Lunge?
To receive verification, you must upload proof of your trainer certification(s). Proof may be submitted using one of the following:
  • The official organization certificate, scanned and uploaded in .pdf format
  • A copy of a letter form the issuing organization containing your full name, date of issuance, and date of certification expiration.
How long does it take for my certification to be approved?
Approval can take anywhere from 1 to 10 business days depending on the certification and method of verification used.
What do I need to do after submitting my documents?
Once you've submitted your documents, there is no further action necessary. Lunge will approve and reject your certification in the order it was submitted
Why can't I upload any more documents?
There is a three document limit to each certification. If you need to submit another file, you should delete one before continuing.
Stil have questions?
Drop us a line at certifications@golunge.com and we'll do our best to answer any other questions you might have.
